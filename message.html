The Message

It is better to put your faith in God (the eternal Goodness and Love that you have inside you) than have confidence in man who leans on his own understanding and trusts only in himself. Rather, seek he who has all his faith and life in God so that you may be connected with him beyond flesh and worldly definitions but through Spirit.
